Our Mission

Intrn.co is an open source community that provides ambitious early-stage startups with the best university talent.

For businesses, our platform naturally vets candidates by requiring a challenge to be completed for every job application. Challenges ensure that only the most qualified and motivated candidates apply for a role, while acting as valuable case studies for applicants who donâ€™t advance.

For students, we curate local, young, and promising startups where one can play an influential role in building a high-growth product. Working with these startups means students get the chance to experience the hustle of building a real business while expanding their skillsets and portfolios.

Intrn.co is built by a group of individuals that recognizes the value of an early-career in startups. We wanted to raise awareness for local startup opportunities while bringing better workers to the local startup community. Interested in brining intrn.co to your school or community? Contact us at support@intrn.co.
